Generation Z (Gen Z)

Generation Z, often referred to as Gen Z, is the demographic cohort succeeding Millennials. They are born between the mid-1990s and the early 2010s. Gen Z is known for its digital nativism, being the first generation to grow up with widespread access to the Internet and technology from a young age.

Characteristics of Gen Z include adaptability, entrepreneurial spirit, tech-savviness, and a strong sense of social justice. They are diverse, open-minded, and value inclusivity.
